# Product Overview

This project provides comprehensive documentation and integration resources for Range's blockchain intelligence platform. Range offers enterprise-grade APIs for:

- **Data API**: Blockchain intelligence across 18+ networks with unified access to addresses, transactions, and protocol analytics
- **Faraday API**: Stablecoin transfer infrastructure with built-in routing, compliance (IVMS101), and risk management
- **Risk API**: Advanced threat detection, sanctions screening, and transaction risk scoring using ML models

The platform serves enterprises managing $35B+ in crypto assets, providing critical compliance, risk assessment, and operational intelligence for digital asset operations.

## Key Use Cases
- Cross-chain stablecoin transfers with compliance
- Address and transaction risk scoring
- Blockchain data analytics and monitoring
- OFAC sanctions and blacklist screening
- Travel Rule compliance (IVMS101)
